There are crowds of people going in and out of the entrance to the tower. There is nobody checking them at the entrance to the elevator.

The elevator is a large, organic shell-looking surface lifted up by gastropod-type creatures on the walls. They look like large, flat snails. 

There are [[SECURITY GUARDS]] at the entrance to the foyer who are checking guests. The guests are annoyed by this, but they say they can't take any chances.

The foyer is a moderately large room, filled with people trying to get into the party. The walls are lined with mountains of expensive and odd-looking coats and hats. 

The vents are just for air conditioning. They're grown into the walls, so they're fairly large and can be expanded a little without problems considering their organic structure. 

The first room with people in it is a bar. The bar stretches across the entire enormous room, staffed by about ten stressed-looking elves. Staff are constantly rushing into a door behind the bar that seems to have something glowing behind it, and returning with drinks and food. 
There are tables and booths all around the room, all of which are filled with people speaking loudly. Topics of conversation if they try to hear:
Toob and Glorp are getting a divorce. Alvin just got laid off. Jerry's wife took the dog. Jalvin just got promoted. Palpatine returned. (Roll a d6)
The bar serves:
The Chef's Special (Unidentifiable soup. Roll wild magic table if consumed)
Tlorpan Borgflarger (cocktail that makes your head explode and reform thirty seconds later)
Rhinoceros (normal macaroni and cheese)
Curly fries (actual caterpillars)
Plargburger (burger that turns you green. tastes sorta like coffee)

Anyone they talk to will be:
Against the target's election (will divulge misinformation about the target or start complaining about them)
For the target's election (will start shilling them)
There cuz it seemed interesting (will be either bored, drunk, or will start infodumping about a random topic)

The kitchen is a standard line cook kitchen. There's portals to the elemental plane of fire underneath all the stoves. If the players go in, they'll probably be ignored because of how fast everything is going, but the cooks will eventually alert the guards if they get in the way too much. 

The second room is the "formal" dining room area. The tables are evenly spaced, and waiters are constantly coming and going through a door in the back. The door glows. Everyone in the room is a massive twat.
